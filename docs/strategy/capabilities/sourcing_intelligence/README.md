# Sourcing Intelligence

Purpose: convert local market and vendor knowledge into reusable sourcing memory.

## Fill Order

| Step | File | What to do |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | [vendor_memory_template.md](vendor_memory_template.md) | Record vendors and local market sources |
| 2 | [product_source_template.md](product_source_template.md) | Record product categories, substitutes, and risks |
| 3 | [progress_and_feedback_loop.md](progress_and_feedback_loop.md) | Track sourcing trips and reusable notes |
| 4 | [success_metrics.md](success_metrics.md) | Review whether sourcing from zero is decreasing |

## How To Analyze

Group sourcing needs by repeated categories: packaging, chocolates, candles, perfume, leather, decor, resin supplies, workshop materials.

## Done For Version 1

Version 1 is ready when the top 10 repeated sourcing categories have at least one known source and one risk note.
