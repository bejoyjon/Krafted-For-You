# Proof And Story Engine

Purpose: turn orders and workshops into visible proof that Krafted For You understands, designs, crafts, and delivers well.

## Fill Order

| Step | File | What to do |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | [brief_to_gift_story_template.md](brief_to_gift_story_template.md) | Convert each strong order into a story |
| 2 | [proof_capture_checklist.md](proof_capture_checklist.md) | Capture assets before, during, and after work |
| 3 | [progress_and_feedback_loop.md](progress_and_feedback_loop.md) | Track proof assets and gaps |
| 4 | [success_metrics.md](success_metrics.md) | Review proof capture monthly |

## How To Analyze

Identify which stories create trust: emotional fit, craft detail, customer reaction, reliable delivery, workshop transformation.

## Done For Version 1

Version 1 is ready when every strong order has at least one story note and one final photo.
