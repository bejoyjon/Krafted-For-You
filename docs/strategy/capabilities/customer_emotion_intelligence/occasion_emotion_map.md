# Occasion Emotion Map

Purpose: define the emotional job of common gifting occasions.

## Template

| Occasion | Primary emotion | Secondary emotion | Useful prompts | Typical risk |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Birthday | Personal celebration | Delight | What is uniquely them? | Becoming generic |
| Anniversary | Remembered love | Intimacy | What shared memory should the gift carry? | Becoming too cliched |
| Farewell | Gratitude | Continuity | What should they remember about this phase? | Becoming too formal |
| New home | Belonging | Warmth | What would make the space feel personal? | Becoming decorative clutter |
| Corporate appreciation | Respect | Taste | What should the recipient infer about the giver? | Becoming generic bulk gifting |

## How To Fill

Use reconstructed past orders first. If no past example exists, keep the row as a hypothesis and mark it for validation.

## How To Analyze

For each occasion, review:

- what customers actually asked for,
- what emotion the founder believes mattered,
- which option paths converted,
- which details made the gift feel personal.

Update the map when customer reactions contradict assumptions.
