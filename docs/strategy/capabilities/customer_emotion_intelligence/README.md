## How To Analyze

1. Separate facts from assumptions.
2. Mark photo-only interpretations as inferred.
3. Look for repeated combinations of occasion, relationship, recipient type, and desired feeling.
4. Convert repeated patterns into archetypes and occasion prompts.
5. Use founder corrections to improve the brief template and agent prompt.

## Done For Version 1

This capability has a usable first version when:

- at least 20 reconstructed records are filled,
- top 5 occasions are mapped,
- top 5 recipient archetypes are defined,
- the emotion brief can be used for every serious new inquiry,
- progress and success metrics are being reviewed monthly.
