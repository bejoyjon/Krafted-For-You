# Proposal Template

Purpose: present options clearly without sounding generic or over-automated.

## Customer Proposal Draft

```text
Based on what you shared, the gift should feel [desired feeling] and should reflect [recipient clue].

I would suggest three directions:

Option 1: [Concept]
Best if you want [intent].
Includes [summary].

Option 2: [Concept]
Best if you want [intent].
Includes [summary].

Option 3: [Concept]
Best if you want [intent].
Includes [summary].

Once you choose the direction, I can refine the details, timeline, and final quote.
```

## How To Fill

Use the customer's own language where possible. Keep the proposal short enough to make the next decision easy.

## How To Analyze

Track:

- whether the customer chooses an option,
- whether they ask for a complete redesign,
- what confused them,
- whether budget or timeline was unclear.

Update the proposal language when customers repeatedly ask the same clarifying question.
