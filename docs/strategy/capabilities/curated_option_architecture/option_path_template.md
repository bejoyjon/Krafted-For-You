## How To Fill

Option A should be elegant and feasible. Option B should maximize personal fit. Option C should be higher-impact, higher-budget, or higher-complexity.

Do not invent final prices or availability. Use budget bands and risk notes until founder confirms feasibility.

## How To Analyze

Track which role gets chosen most often. If customers repeatedly choose Option B, emotional fit is the main driver. If they choose Option A, feasibility or budget may matter more. If Option C never converts, it may be too expensive, too vague, or not premium enough.
