# Order Complexity Tiers

| Tier | Definition | Minimum lead time | Human approval required | Risk flags |
|---|---|---:|---|---|
| Simple | Known format, available materials, light personalization | 3-5 days | Quote and final design | Delivery date under 3 days |
| Medium | Mix of handmade and sourced elements, moderate personalization | 7-10 days | Concept, quote, sourcing | New vendor, unclear budget |
| Complex | New handmade format, rare sourcing, multiple approvals | 14+ days | Full order plan | Unproven material, fixed event date |

## How To Fill

Assign every order a tier before quoting. If unsure, assign the higher tier.

## How To Analyze

Monthly, compare tier against actual effort. If "simple" orders repeatedly become stressful, change the tier rules.
