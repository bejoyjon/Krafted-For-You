### Summary

- Format: `Reel`
- Pillar: `Founder and brand story`

### Hook Options
- `Personalised gifts should feel like a memory, not a box.`
- `This is why I started Krafted For You.`

### 20-25 Second Script
`Hi, I am Advika, the founder of Krafted For You.`

`I started this page because I believe gifts should feel personal, thoughtful, and full of meaning.`

`Here, I create customised hampers, creative gift ideas, and art workshops for people who want celebrations to feel more special.`

`If you love meaningful gifting and handmade creativity, follow along.`

### Shorter 12-15 Second Version
`Hi, I am Advika.`

`I create customised hampers and art experiences that make gifting feel personal again.`

`Follow Krafted For You for thoughtful gift ideas and creative inspiration.`

### Shot List
1. Founder looking into camera with natural light
2. Close-up of hands arranging hamper elements
3. Founder smiling or working at the table
4. Quick workshop clip or finished artwork
5. Finished hamper hero shot
6. Founder back to camera for CTA

### On-Screen Text
- Frame 1: `Meaningful gifts > generic gifts`
- Frame 2: `Hi, I'm Advika`
- Frame 3: `Custom hampers`
- Frame 4: `Art workshops`
- Frame 5: `Made with care in Bangalore`
- Final frame: `Follow @kraftedforyou198_`

### Caption Draft
`Hi, I'm Advika, the heart behind Krafted For You.`

`This page is where personalised gifting meets creativity. From customised hampers to hands-on art workshops, everything here is designed to make celebrations feel more thoughtful and memorable.`

`If you love meaningful gifts and creative experiences, you are in the right place.`

`Follow along for gift ideas, workshop updates, and behind-the-scenes moments.`

### Visual Direction
- Shoot near a window, not under harsh white lighting.
- Wear a solid color that complements maroon, cream, or warm yellow.
- Keep the background clean: craft desk, ribbon, flowers, finished hamper, sketchbook.
- Use at least one close-up and one medium founder shot.

### Audio Direction
- Best fit: soft piano and cello, or warm acoustic instrumental
- Search cues: `soft acoustic instrumental`, `piano cello calm`
- Volume:
  - voice at `100%`,
  - music at `15-20%`

### Editing Notes
- Keep cuts quick, about `1-2 seconds` each.
- Add subtitles for every spoken line.
- Make the cover image the founder face plus the headline: `Why Krafted For You Exists`

## Post 2

### Summary
- Date: `2026-05-20`
- Format: `Carousel`
- Pillar: `Gift use cases`
- Objective: `saves and shares`
- CTA: `Save this for your next gifting moment`

### Core Idea
This is meant to create a save-worthy utility post. Saves are currently weak, so this should be designed as reference content.

### Carousel Title
`5 times a custom hamper beats a generic gift`

### Slide Flow
1. Cover: `5 times a custom hamper beats a generic gift`
2. `Birthday gifts when you want it to feel personal`
3. `Farewell gifts when one item is not enough`
4. `Anniversary gifts when memories matter more than price`
5. `Bridesmaid or wedding gifting when you want a cohesive look`
6. `Corporate gifting when you want people to actually remember your brand`
7. Final CTA: `Save this post. DM "HAMPER" to create your own.`

### Caption Draft
`A custom hamper works best when the gift needs to feel personal, intentional, and memorable.`

`Here are 5 situations where it can do much more than a generic off-the-shelf gift.`

`If you are planning a birthday, farewell, wedding, anniversary, or corporate gift, save this for later.`

`DM "HAMPER" if you want us to create one for your occasion.`

### Visual Direction
- Use one clean background per slide, not a different noisy design each time.
- Combine:
  - one strong headline,
  - one supporting line,
  - one relevant hamper or styling image.
- Use the same font and text placement on every slide.
- Keep the cover slide bold and clean.

### Audio Direction
Carousels do not depend much on audio, but if converted into a video carousel or repost story:
- use warm acoustic instrumental,
- keep it light and non-distracting.

### Design Notes
- Do not overload slides with paragraphs.
- Aim for `8-15 words` per main slide.
- Use gift visuals that show outcomes, not just packaging.
- Cover suggestion: maroon background, cream serif headline, one premium hamper image.

## Post 3

### Summary
- Date: `2026-05-22`
- Format: `Reel`
- Pillar: `Behind the scenes / craft process`
- Objective: `shares, reach, and workshop inquiries`
- CTA: `DM "WORKSHOP" if you want to learn this`

### Core Idea
This uses the pipe-cleaner flower direction already visible on the account, but turns it into a clearer process reel with a stronger viewer payoff.

### Hook Options
- `You would never guess this flower is made from pipe cleaners.`
- `From craft wire to gift-worthy sunflower in seconds.`
- `This starts as a childhood craft supply and ends as a beautiful handmade piece.`

### 15-20 Second Voiceover Script
`This sunflower started with simple pipe cleaners.`

`A few twists, layers, and shaping steps later, it became a handmade piece that feels playful, personal, and gift-worthy.`

`If you want to learn projects like this in a workshop, DM "WORKSHOP".`

### No-Voice Alternative
Use only on-screen text:
- `Made from pipe cleaners`
- `Twist`
- `Shape`
- `Layer`
- `Finish`
- `Gift-worthy result`
- `DM "WORKSHOP" to learn this`

### Shot List
1. Materials laid out neatly
2. Hand picking up pipe cleaners
3. Twisting or shaping close-up
4. Petal formation
5. Final assembly
6. Finished sunflower in hand
7. Optional clip of it styled as gift add-on or workshop sample

### Caption Draft
`Plot twist: this sunflower started as pipe cleaners.`

`This is the kind of simple-looking material that can turn into something playful, handmade, and surprisingly gift-worthy.`

`Would you like to learn this in a workshop? DM "WORKSHOP".`

### Visual Direction
- Shoot on a clean tabletop with daylight.
- Use overhead and close-up shots.
- Keep the background neutral so the yellow flower stands out.
- End with one hero shot in natural greenery or with wrapped gifting props.

### Audio Direction
- Best fit: light lo-fi or soft upbeat acoustic
- Search cues: `gentle indie instrumental`, `lofi cozy`, `ambient craft sounds`
- Optional approach:
  - keep the natural twisting and paper sounds,
  - layer very soft music beneath them.

### Editing Notes
- Keep the final result visible by second `3`.
- Use jump cuts, not long process footage.
- Put the best-looking finished shot both at the beginning and the end.
- Cover headline: `Made From Pipe Cleaners?`

## Production Checklist For All 3 Posts

- Shoot all 3 within one half-day to reduce setup cost.
- Record clean founder voice in a quiet room using phone mic close to mouth.
- Use the same color grade and subtitle style on all reels.
- Export reel covers separately so the top 9 grid looks intentional.
- Keep one CTA only per post.
- After posting, log reach, shares, saves, follows, and DMs in the KPI sheet within `72` hours.
