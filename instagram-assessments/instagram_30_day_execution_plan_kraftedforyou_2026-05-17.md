- increase posting consistency from `2.43 posts per 30 days` to `16 posts in 30 days`,

### Visual System For The Reset
- Primary colors: the existing maroon and warm yellow, with cream or off-white backgrounds.
- Accent colors: muted blush pink, warm beige, leaf green in props only.
- Typography:

  - support font: clean sans-serif for small explanatory text.

  - one hero image or founder face,
  - minimal extra text,
  - same logo placement across covers.
- Ratio:
  - reels: shoot `9:16`,
  - carousels: design `4:5`.

## 5. Content Production Rules

### Reel Structure
- First `1.5` seconds: hook on screen and visually obvious subject.
- Middle: show process, founder, or result quickly.
- End: single CTA on screen and in caption.

### Carousel Structure
- Slide 1: problem or curiosity headline.
- Slides 2-5: examples, steps, or proof.
- Final slide: CTA and DM keyword.

### Story Structure
- Frame 1: attention or setup
- Frame 2: value, proof, or process
- Frame 3: interaction sticker or CTA

## 6. Audio Direction

Do not over-optimize for "trending" audio at the expense of brand fit. Use repeatable audio styles that match the brand:

### Best Audio Types For This Brand
- Warm acoustic instrumental
- Soft piano and cello
- Light indie folk guitar
- Lo-fi beat with soft percussion
- Natural craft sounds plus low music bed

### Audio Search Cues To Use Inside Instagram
- `soft acoustic instrumental`
- `piano cello calm`
- `lofi cozy`
- `gentle indie instrumental`
- `ambient craft sounds`

### Match Audio To Content Type
- Founder reels: piano, cello, or acoustic guitar
- BTS reels: natural foley plus minimal beat
- Product reveal reels: warm instrumental with a gentle drop
- Workshop reels: brighter indie acoustic or light upbeat instrumental

## 7. Operational Checklist

For every post:
- write the hook before filming,
- decide one CTA only,
- add one explicit reason to save, share, or DM,
- reshare to stories within 30 minutes,
- reply to every comment within 24 hours,
- log the post URL and key insights in the KPI sheet after 48-72 hours.

## 8. Success Markers For This 30-Day Plan

By `2026-06-16`, aim for:
- `16` feed posts published,
- at least `3` posts with `3+` shares,
- at least `4` posts with `2+` saves,
- at least `3` posts that generate `2+` follows,
- clear top-performing pillar data across founder, workshop, proof, and gifting use-case content.
