## 3. What The Data Says

### Strengths
- Founder-led content performs best. The `2026-05-13` founder reel produced the strongest recent all-around result: `1,336` views, `707` reach, `50` interactions, `6` shares, and `3` follows.
- The account can reach non-followers. Reels and older introduction posts show there is discoverability potential beyond the current follower base.
- The offer is already broad enough to support multiple content pillars: hampers, bespoke gifting, workshops, founder story, and creative process.

### Weaknesses
- Publishing consistency is weak. The account is not posting often enough or steadily enough to build audience memory.
- Comments are low. The recent sample averages under `1` comment per post.
- Saves are almost absent. Across the recent 6-post sample, there was only `1` save.
- Most posts do not drive profile actions. Outside the March 6 outlier and the May 13 founder reel, profile activity and follows are near zero.
- Visual and message consistency is still mixed. Some posts feel branded and intentional; others feel like one-off uploads.

### Interpretation
- The page has brand potential, but it is not yet operating like a content system.
- The audience seems more responsive to human story and identity than to plain product display.
- Reels are useful for discovery, but not all reels are working. The best reel had a face, a clear voice, and a simple brand introduction. The weaker reels were generic or visually less sticky.
- Product posts can still convert, but they need stronger hooks, clearer use cases, and more proof.

## 4. Strategy For The Next 90 Days

### Strategic Goal
Turn the page from a portfolio feed into a demand-generation channel that consistently produces:
- discovery through reels,
- trust through founder and workshop content,
- conversion through gifting proof and offers.

### Primary Objectives
1. Raise posting consistency from `2.43 posts per 30 days` to at least `12 posts per 30 days`.
2. Lift engagement by reach from `5.37%` to `7%+`.
3. Increase average shares from `1.67` to `3+` per post.
4. Increase average saves from `0.17` to `2+` per post.
5. Increase average follows per post from `1.0` to `2+`.
6. Start measuring direct business outcomes: DMs, WhatsApp clicks, workshop inquiries, and orders from Instagram.

### Positioning
Use one clear positioning sentence repeatedly:

`Krafted For You creates personalised gift hampers and hands-on art experiences that make celebrations feel personal, thoughtful, and memorable.`

### Content Pillars
1. Founder and brand story
   Goal: trust, recall, follows
   Examples: why Krafted exists, who Advika is, how ideas become products, workshop philosophy

2. Gift outcomes and customer use cases
   Goal: conversion
   Examples: boyfriend hamper ideas, wedding gifting, farewell hampers, birthday custom gifts, corporate gifting

3. Behind-the-scenes craftsmanship
   Goal: authenticity and differentiation
   Examples: packing process, materials, personalization choices, time-lapse assembly, sourcing details

4. Workshops and creative education
   Goal: saves, shares, local leads
   Examples: beginner art tips, workshop snippets, weekend invites, student outputs, FAQ clips

5. Social proof and trust
   Goal: conversion
   Examples: testimonials, customer reactions, delivery photos, workshop participant feedback, before/after gifting stories

### Recommended Cadence
- Reels: `3` per week
- Carousels/static posts: `2` per week
- Stories: daily or near-daily
- Founder face-on-camera content: at least `1` per week
- Offer-led conversion post: at least `1` per week

### Creative Rules
- Use face + voice regularly. The current best recent post is founder-led.
- Start with a one-line hook in the first second or first slide.
- Reduce text-heavy tiles unless they are carousel covers with a strong curiosity hook.
- Show the outcome, not only the object.
- Add one CTA per post only: `DM to order`, `Save this`, `Share with someone`, or `Join the workshop`.
- Build visual consistency around one typography system and one cover style.

### Funnel Design
- Discovery content: reels, transformation, founder clips, workshop snippets
- Trust content: BTS, testimonials, process, founder story, FAQ
- Conversion content: pricing range cues, gifting use cases, delivery deadlines, workshop dates, clear inquiry CTA

### Engagement System
- Reply to every comment within `24 hours`
- Use stories to reshare each post and ask one response-driving question
- End at least half of posts with a prompt that invites comments
- DM warm responders who ask about price, dates, customization, or workshop slots

### Conversion System
- Standardize CTA paths:
  - hampers -> `DM "HAMPER"`
  - workshops -> `DM "WORKSHOP"`
  - corporate gifting -> `DM "CORPORATE"`
- Track every inquiry in a simple sheet with source post URL and final outcome
- Add offer-specific story highlights: `Hampers`, `Workshops`, `Reviews`, `Corporate`

### 30-Day Experiment Plan
1. Run `4` founder-led reels with strong hooks
2. Run `4` product/use-case reels with direct CTA
3. Publish `4` testimonial or proof-led posts
4. Publish `4` educational or workshop-led pieces designed for saves
5. Compare by:
   - reach
   - shares
   - saves
   - follows
   - DMs

## 5. KPI Scorecard Guidance

Use the CSV template in [instagram_kpi_scorecard_template.csv](./instagram_kpi_scorecard_template.csv).

### Core KPI Definitions
- Content consistency percent = `published posts / planned posts * 100`
- Engagement by followers percent = `(likes + comments + shares + saves) / followers * 100`
- Engagement by reach percent = `(likes + comments + shares + saves) / reach * 100`
- Follow conversion per post = `follows generated by post`
- Lead conversion percent = `orders from Instagram / qualified Instagram inquiries * 100`

### Metrics To Watch Weekly
- Posts published
- Reels published
- Story days active
- Average reach
- Average shares
- Average saves
- Average follows per post
- DMs generated
- WhatsApp clicks

### Metrics To Watch Monthly
- Net follower growth
- Engagement by reach
- Content pillar mix
- Top 3 posts by follows
- Top 3 posts by saves
- Orders and revenue attributed to Instagram

3. Prioritize founder-led reels and testimonial-led content because the current data suggests those formats can move people more than generic product posts.
