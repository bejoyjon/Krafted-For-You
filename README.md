# About Krafted for You 
I am a small gifting business entrepreneur, whose core focus is on delivering bespoke hampers to my customer. This bespoke hamper can contain almost anything they want. E.g. a customer wanted a hamper with her boyfriend's favorite soap, a cologne that we suggested, imported chocolates and custom made leather sandals. What I specialize in is creating handmade, custom art decor - resin art, wax art, texture art and continuing to add to this portfolio. I have a relatively non-technical background.
