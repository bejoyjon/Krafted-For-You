# About Krafted for You 
I am a small gifting business entrepreneur, whose core focus is on delivering bespoke hampers to my customer. This bespoke hamper can contain almost anything they want. E.g. a customer wanted a hamper with her boyfriend's favorite soap, a cologne that we suggested, imported chocolates and custom made leather sandals. What I specialize in is creating handmade, custom art decor - resin art, wax art, texture art and continuing to add to this portfolio and over the past 10 years. I have a relatively non-technical background.

## Vision 
I want Krafted For You to be "top of the mind" brand when it comes to customized gifting, because of our ability to custom make any kind and any size of gifts for any occasion reliably.

## Value proposition\elevator pitch
My customers value Krafted For You because we deliver on quality (creative yet sophisticated designs, any level of customizations, high quality raw material, extreme focus on product detail, often handmade, shipped with sufficient protection to prevent quality degradation), time (always on time delivery) and great customer buying experience (quick responses to customer enquiries, custom requests narrowed down to options very quickly, order to cash made very easy).

## The team
I am a artist-soloprenueur who handles everything from product conception, order management, marketing to sourcing and shipping. Due to the seasonal/sporadic nature of my business, I need to be highly reliable when it comes to the core product and extremely cost efficient on the overheads.

## How the business runs currently 

### Order management
Post on Instagram --> Customer enquires on DM --> conversation on various product options and pricing --> Order confirmed with advance payment --> product build --> delivery and final payment

### Product development
#### Internal products
Once I get the order, I order in raw material and other "tooling" (like silicon moulds, hot glue guns....) in case I don't already have it in my stock. My real value addition is my experience and insights received from years of experimenting and creating everyday art, and using them to make handmade products which are exquisite, made with great care to detail and aesthetics. This product creation is done after rounds of design conversation with the client. 

#### External products
External products can be of two types - One, commercial products which are pre-made such as perfumes, shirts, chocolates, art decor and candles. The other type is handmade products created by other artists like me.

### Workshops
To boost the market for customized, handmade products, we host workshops to bring awareness on handmade products. Workshops follow this cycel: Posts on Instagram --> Registrations --> workshops.

### Marketing
Posts on Insta, word of mouth from existing customer base.

- We want to create a brand image that communicates sophistication, class and limitles possibilities, by improving our digital and offline presence.
